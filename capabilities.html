Operational Strategy & Advisory

Core Capabilities

We deliver deep operational advisory, process engineering, and tactical training programs to secure carrier pipelines before, during, and after major catastrophe events.

Claims Consulting & Advisory

We provide senior executive guidance to streamline claims pipelines, align procurement structures with carrier operations, and manage large-scale dispute resolution workflows.

✓ Enterprise Procurement Alignment: Structuring and optimizing vendor relationships, independent adjusting firm SLA frameworks, and software integrations.
✓ Litigation & Appraisal Engineering: Implementing defensible, structured processes to manage disputed property claims and transfer litigation exposures to appraisal tracks.
✓ Predictive Resource Modeling: Projecting field deployment requirements and claims volume scaling metrics based on historic storm models.
✓ Enterprise Business Continuity: Formulating disaster recovery protocols and executive-level command structures for severe CAT scenarios.

Strategic Process Optimization

We engineer operational frameworks, design clean data capture loops, and customize claims processing workflows for specialized perils and challenging geographic regions.

✓ System Architecture & Data Capture: Designing clean integration pipelines between intake systems, field estimating tools, and core carrier databases.
✓ CAT Code Engineering: Establishing storm-specific process guidelines, estimate templates, and automated compliance auditing rules.
✓ Niche Product Customization: Engineering specialized processing pipelines for unique policy types, including manufactured housing and mobile home product lines.
✓ Logistical Conflict Resolution: Developing localized, tactical response workflows for hard-to-reach barrier islands, keys, and severe access-restricted disaster zones (e.g., Sanibel Island and the Florida Keys).

Training & Operational Readiness

We build readiness programs, technical estimating academies, and automated onboarding playbooks to scale up human capital ahead of historic catastrophe events.

✓ Leadership Development Academies: Targeted executive training programs for Team Leads, Field Supervisors, Quality Control Auditors, and Claims Managers.
✓ Technical Estimating Mastery: Advanced training curricula in Xactimate and Symbility, emphasizing structural estimating precision and carrier-specific guidelines.
✓ Massive-Scale Readiness Drills: Conducting stress-test simulations of staffing ramp-ups, QA queues, and high-frequency intake bottlenecks.
✓ Rapid Onboarding Systems: Creating high-impact independent adjuster orientation presentation playbooks and onboarding resources to ensure day-one operational alignment.
